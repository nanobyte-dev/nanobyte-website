Thank you for your interest in Nanobyte! There are several ways you can contribute to our community and help us grow.

## Contributing to the Website

The Nanobyte website is open source and welcomes contributions! Whether you want to fix a typo, improve documentation, or add new content, we'd love your help.

**GitHub Repository:** [github.com/nanobyte-dev/nanobyte-website](https://github.com/nanobyte-dev/nanobyte-website)

### How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub
2. **Clone your fork** to your local machine
3. **Create a new branch** for your changes
4. **Make your changes** - Fix bugs, improve content, add features
5. **Test your changes** - Run both modern and legacy builds
6. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description of your changes

### What We're Looking For

- **Content improvements** - Fix typos, clarify explanations, add examples
- **New tutorials** - Share your knowledge about OS development
- **Bug fixes** - Fix issues with the site or theme
- **Feature additions** - Improve the user experience
- **Documentation** - Help others understand how things work

See the [README](https://github.com/nanobyte-dev/nanobyte-website#readme) for development setup instructions.

## Supporting Us on Patreon

If you enjoy our content and want to support the creation of more tutorials, videos, and educational materials, consider becoming a patron!

**Support us on Patreon:** [patreon.com/nanobyte](https://www.patreon.com/nanobyte)

Your support helps us:
- Dedicate more time to creating quality content
- Cover hosting and infrastructure costs
- Invest in better tools and equipment
- Keep all content free and accessible to everyone

## Other Ways to Help

### Spread the Word

- **Share our content** on social media
- **Recommend Nanobyte** to friends learning about OS development
- **Star our repository** on GitHub to show your support

### Join the Community

- **Discord:** [Join our community](https://discord.gg/xNrRVXtsgs) - Ask questions, share your projects, help others
- **YouTube:** [Subscribe to our channel](https://www.youtube.com/@nanobyte-dev) - Watch tutorials and get notified of new content

### Report Issues

Found a bug or have a suggestion? [Open an issue](https://github.com/nanobyte-dev/nanobyte-website/issues) on GitHub!
